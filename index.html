Corporate Overview

A trusted leader in the global energy market, connecting resources to where they are needed most.

Established in 2018, CHANGSUNG KOREA CO., LTD. has rapidly grown into a specialized global oil trading and eco-friendly enterprise. Based in Seoul, South Korea, we secure highly competitive petroleum products through robust global networks and supply them efficiently to international markets. Our commitment to transparency, quality, and sustainable practices drives our vision to build long-term partnerships worldwide.

Core Business Pillars

Optimizing the global supply chain across crude oil, petroleum products, and logistics.

🛢️

Crude Oil Trading

We leverage deep relationships with oil-producing nations to secure a stable supply of high-quality crude oil. Our strategic trading ensures consistent delivery to designated global refineries under the most optimal terms.

⛽

Petroleum Products

We specialize in the efficient distribution of refined petrochemical products. By capitalizing on regional supply and demand imbalances, we supply key products via optimized logistics channels globally.

🚢

Logistics & Bunkering

Our integrated logistics network includes comprehensive offshore bunkering and large-scale terminal storage operations, designed to maximize supply chain flexibility and structurally mitigate market volatility.
