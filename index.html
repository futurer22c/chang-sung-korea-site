About Chungsung Korea

Established in 2018, Chungsung Korea was born to meet the demands of the ever-changing global energy market. Building on the successful partnership and experience of GEMS HABIL, Chungsung Korea was founded under the leadership of Chairman Kim Jung-han. We are constantly researching and developing to pioneer new markets and meet differentiated customer expectations through close cooperation with global oil refineries. Our goal is to go beyond simply supplying petroleum products and to grow together with our customers based on innovative solutions and trust.
